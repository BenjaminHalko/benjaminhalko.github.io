Key Features

  • Crushing Weight Your hammer never stops growing! Make it to the next magical grindstone to reduce it down before it's too late.
  • Hit the Floor Whack the ground to vault yourself through the environment at blistering speeds; then bunny-hop to preserve your pace!
  • Scale-Based Combat Scale up your Hammer for devastating heavy attacks or keep it light-weight and unleash a flurry of blows!
  • Campaign Mode Level-based progression through the wizard's tower with increasing challenges.
  • Speedrun Leaderboards Steam integration for global time comparisons. Compete against players worldwide!
  • Accessibility Options Adjustable game speed, customizable controls, font selection, UI scaling, high contrast modes, and reduced distraction settings.
