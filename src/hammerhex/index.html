About the Game

Wield a continuously growing magical hammer to navigate through levels while managing its expanding size. A curse from a dying wizard causes your magical hammer to grow uncontrollably. You must find grindstones to shrink it while fighting through a wizard's tower to escape.

Time your swings carefully, preserve momentum through bunny-hopping, and balance hammer size against combat effectiveness as you race through the tower!

Features

  • Campaign: Level-based progression through the wizard's tower
  • Scale-Based Combat: Larger hammers deliver powerful attacks; smaller ones enable rapid strikes
  • Speed & Momentum: Bunny-hopping mechanics reward skillful play
  • Leaderboards: Steam integration for global time comparisons
  • Accessibility: Adjustable game speed, customizable controls, font selection, UI scaling, high contrast modes, and reduced distraction settings
